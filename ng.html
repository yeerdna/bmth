POST HUMAN: NeX GEn

"...and my ego is not my amigo..."

POST HUMAN: NeX GEn is the seventh studio album by the British rock band Bring Me the Horizon. It was surprise released on 24 May 2024, originally planned for 15 September 2023, as the second installment of the POST HUMAN series, following POST HUMAN: SURVIVAL HORROR (2020). It is also the last album to feature band keyboardist and supporting producer Jordan Fish, as he departed from the group in late 2023.
